## Officially Supported

Distro                                          | Package Maintainer
------------------------------------------------|-----------------------|
Ubuntu 16.04 "Xenial" or later `*`              | [lah7]
Debian 8 "Jessie" or later                      | [lah7]
Arch Linux                                      | [z3ntu]
Fedora 27 or later                              | [z3ntu]
openSUSE Leap 15.0 or Tumbleweed                | [z3ntu]
Gentoo                                          | [vifino]
Red Hat / CentOS `**`                           | [moozhub]

`*` Includes flavours and variants (such as Linux Mint)

`**` OpenRazer (and Polychromatic) does not officially support Red Hat or CentOS right now ([#399], [#408]).
However, [moozhub has created packages](https://github.com/moozhub/yum-repo-mooz) you can use.
If you have issues, please contact the package maintainer.
